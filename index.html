A Little Bit About Me...

Im a passionate web developer with experience in front end development where I use HTML, CSS, Javascript and a multitude of other languages and frameworks to craft responsive and visually engaging websites. From an early age I took a keen fascination with computers and the internet and over time my interest in the topic only grew, eventually leading to me completing my Master Degree in Computer Science. At first I would create Wordpress and Shopify websites both for clients and for my own personal projects, before then progressing to coding my own websites completely from scratch.

In recent years I have spent time gaining experience working as a Web Developer. As well as coding, I have often spent time in my recent roles on SEO, image editing, video editing, crafting effective and appealing product listings and descriptions, working within a team to bring a vision to life and pretty much everything else it takes to create and maintain successful websites.

Nowadays when I get free time I enjoy working on my own personal projects and completing Javascript coding challenges that require me to use my own logic, math skills and knowledge of algorithms to solve.
